Things I Love

MotorSport

Motorsport is my passion, and I thrive on the excitement and diversity it brings. From the high-speed precision of Formula 1 to the endurance battles of IMSA and WEC, and the raw intensity of NASCAR, I find inspiration in every corner of the racing world. Whether it’s cutting-edge technology, strategic brilliance, or the pure thrill of competition, motorsport fuels my enthusiasm and connects me with a global community of fans and teams.

IT and Technology

Technology is at the core of my curiosity and creativity. I’m passionate about exploring all aspects of IT, from the complexities of infrastructure to the transformative potential of generative AI, like the tools I use every day. Among my interests, security stands out as a critical focus, where I’m driven to understand and address the challenges of safeguarding digital environments in an ever-evolving technological landscape.

Football and Sports

I have a deep passion for competitive sports, embracing the thrill and camaraderie they bring. Whether it’s cheering on Liverpool in football, rooting for the Steelers in the NFL, or enjoying the precision and strategy of golf and darts, I find joy in the diversity of competition. From skiing down slopes to following almost every sport in between, I thrive on the energy, skill, and excitement that make each game and match unique.
